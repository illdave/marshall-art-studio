Sketchbook

Most of my sketches are pubic observational drawings made with ink-on-paper media (usually without a pencil phase). Digital sketches done on a Wacom tablet with Photoshop, Painter, and Procreate. Once upon a time I even used TealPaint. I sketch people in restaurants, coffee shops, or Agile daily standup meetings when I should be focused on closing the sprint. My work is mostly representational, but sometimes imaginative and occasionally collaborative.

Digital

Drawn in Corel Painter and/or Adobe Photoshop with a Wacom tablet. This would've been in the early 2000s. These were created in various aspect ratios, but presented here in 9 x 12 inch sketchbook sizes for web UX consistency.

TealPaint

Sketches done in TealPaint, a PalmOS app on my old Treo. These images aren't clickable since there's no way to make 160 x 146 pixel images look good in a modern web environment.
