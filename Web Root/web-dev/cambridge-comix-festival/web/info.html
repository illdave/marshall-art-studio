So, What Is the Cambridge Comix Festival?
The Cambridge Comix Festival is an opportunity for Boston/Cambridge Area cartoonists to demonstrate their abilities to the public. The Festival includes exhibitions of artwork, as well as a convention where the you can personally meet the artists, and possibly buy their work. It is a chance for people to discover what talents are lurking in their midst and learn about the vast potentials of comics as expressive art. "Where is this event" you ask?
This event will open to the public, free of charge. The convention will include guest speakers and video demonstrations. Artwork by local cartoonists will be on exhibit during the month of May 2003 at the following Cambridge locations:

How Can I Join?
The Cambridge Comix Festival is open to submissions from cartoonists throughout New England. We are seeking artwork of any and all types of subject matter. Artwork for exhibition must be two-dimensional and ready to hang. Table space at the convention is limited, so please reserve a space as soon as possible. Membership is $25. Please be aware that all funds solicited are intended for the sole purpose of financing this event. This is a non-profit organization. Deadlines and contact information can be found on this page.
