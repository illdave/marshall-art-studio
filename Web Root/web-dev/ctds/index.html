Connecticut Digital Service

Senior UX/UI Front-End Developer and Designer, 2021-2015 Designed and developed accessible government websites serving millions of Connecticut residents. Built custom Sitecore CMS components, created design system standards, and collaborated with state agencies through Agile workflows. Provided UX design using Figma, accessibility consulting, graphics production, and technical training.

Agencies served: Clean Slate, Department of Administrative Services (DAS), Department of Developmental Services (DDS), Department of Labor (DOL), Department of Transportation (DOT), Department of Public Health (DPH), State Board of Education (EdSight)

Technologies: HTML5, CSS3, SCSS, Bootstrap, Sitecore CMS, Figma, Adobe XD, JIRA, Agile, JotForms

Key Contributions

  • Designed and implemented responsive, WCAG-compliant components
  • Created custom data table code snippets for complex state information
  • Established UX and accessibility best practices across multiple agencies
  • Developed training materials and documentation for Sitecore CMS
  • Collaborated across design, development, and content teams using Agile methodology
  • Delivered projects on tight deadlines while maintaining quality standards
