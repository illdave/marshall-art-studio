About UnfairDenial.com

People buy insurance for one basic purpose - to be protected against loss. Yet some insurance companies have failed to investigate claims, delayed investigations, and failed to pay claims based on a misrepresentation of the language in the insurance contract. Every year, hundreds of claims may be denied without justification.

When you buy disability insurance, you put your faith in your insurer. When a claim is unjustifiably denied, it can have devastating effects on the insured and their families.

What If Your Claim is Denied?

Unfortunately, consumer protection for insured parties whose claims have been denied is virtually non-existent. There are no federal statutes regulating the insurance industry. Although all states have insurance departments and most have enacted legislation outlawing unfair insurance practices, they offer little in the way of enforcement and no compensation for the denied claimants.
