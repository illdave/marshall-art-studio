Eddie Marshall

Web Design and Development, 2009 Complete redesign of marketing website for internationally acclaimed jazz drummer Eddie Marshall. Collaborated with artist's management to overhaul information architecture and visual design, improving usability, legibility, and search engine optimization.

Rebranded site with sophisticated color palette, integrated Google Analytics for performance tracking, and shifted editorial voice from casual to archival tone appropriate for documenting a distinguished musical career. Project completed over one-month timeline.
