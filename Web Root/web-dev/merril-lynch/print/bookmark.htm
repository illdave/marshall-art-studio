Cross-Promotional Bookmark

Print Design | 2001 Co-branded bookmark created in partnership with Quantum Books, an MIT-area technical bookstore. Developed concept collaboratively with client, then designed layout, created illustration, and established typography treatment.

Additional work included researching literary quotes for content, reverse-engineering Quantum Books logo into vector format, and managing print production. Successful campaign satisfied both Quantum Books and Merrill Lynch ITG stakeholders.
