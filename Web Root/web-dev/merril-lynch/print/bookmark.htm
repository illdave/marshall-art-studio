Cross Promotional Bookmark

2001 Cross-promotional advertising with Quantum Books, an MIT-friendly bookstore. The concept was created by brainstorming with the client. I provided the visual design, the illustration and font-color treatment. In addition, I also researched the quote sources and formatted the Quantumn Books logo into vector are by backwards-engineering. Both Quantumn and Merrill Lynch were pleased with the end results.
