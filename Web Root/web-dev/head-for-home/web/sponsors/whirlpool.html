The Whirlpool® ENERGY-STAR-rated Resource Saver Washer
and The Whirlpool Dryer

The Whirlpool Resource Saver™ washer is the first top loading conventional washer to be ENERGY STAR®-rated. This high-efficiency clothes washer conserves water and energy and saves money on utility bills. This washer saves up to 56% on energy usage and 47% on water usage due to a unique water temperature sensor and spray rinse system. While conserving water and energy, the Resource Saver does not compromise on performance or capacity. This washer has outstanding cleaning, a hand washables cycle that is extra gentle on delicate items and its 3.0 cubic foot wash basket can handle the largest loads.

The Whirlpool dryer features a very large drum capacity and a gentle heat system that is easy on your clothes. It also features a system that periodically tumbles once drying is completed (for up to 45 minutes) to prevent wrinkles from setting in.

Only those products delivering the highest level of energy efficiency can earn the ENERGY STAR from the Department of Energy and Environmental Protection Agency.

That's why your local participating electric and gas utilities have funded and sponsored this important energy efficiency program. They also offer a $100 mail-in rebate on the purchase of high efficiency, ENERGY STAR-rated clothes washers. For more information call 1-877-TumbleW.
