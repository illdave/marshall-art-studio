How to Win

Sweepstakes will run for five consecutive weeks, Monday through Friday only (August 9th through September 10th) (hereinafter referred to as "Sweepstakes Period"). On weekday evenings between 5:00 - 6:00 PM during the Sweepstakes Period, a WBZ on-air personality will invite listeners to call in to the WBZ contest line at (617) 931-1030. The correct numbered caller will automatically win a $100 gift certificate to City Sports ("Daily Winner"). A total of twenty five (25) Daily Winners will be selected during the Sweepstakes Period. Each Daily Winner will be automatically qualified to win all of the Prizes in one of five specific rooms ("Grand Prize Room") in WBZ's Virtual Home, as follows:

  • Week 1: August 9th - 13th (Kitchen)
  • Week 2: August 16th -20th (Dining Room)
  • Week 3 August 23rd - 27th (Living Room)
  • Week 4 August 30th - September 3rd (Master Suite/Bath Room)
  • Week 5: September 6th - 10th (Family Room/Home Office)
