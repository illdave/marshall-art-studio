 Welcome to the official online invitation for the Reunion of the 1980 graduating class of The High School of Commerce, Springfield Massachusetts. Has it really been twenty years since we were the Teenage Dirtbags, Baby?

The top navbar leads to specific information about this year's festivities. The Directions page is for people who've moved away and can't remember the local roadways. If the custom directions don't work for you, there's a helpful link to the amazing Mapquest. Use the Details page to choose a meal, find out what time to show up, and act before the deadline for sending your much-needed, cost-covering check/money order. After your finished there, be sure to register with HighschoolAlumni.com. In addition to acting as an RSVP tracker, you'll find this service offers a lot of functionality (such as emailing other people from our Master List, posting your history, email and URLs) for free that similar outfits make you pay for.

Finally, if you have any questions not answered here, then please send an email to Donna Gregory, the organizer of this year's event.
