Illustration Friday

2003-2020. Illustration Friday was a weekly creative outlet and portfolio builder for illustrators of all levels, founded by Penelope Dullaghan in 2003. After running it for a decade, Dullaghan handed the reins to Illustration Age in March 2013. The site got hijacked by spammers in May 2020, effectively killing it and its unofficial Deviant Art group. Below are my drawings from 2006-2007.
