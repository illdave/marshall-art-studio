Alex Toth critiques Steve Rude

Two masters of storytelling clash!

David Marshall May 4, 2009 April 10, 2025

City of Time Lost

A young Steve Rude faxed pencil art of a Jonny Quest story to Alex Toth for feedback in 1986. Boy, did he get it! I've updated this article since posting it here, moving it to my comics teaching site Art of the Comic Book. New features include a modern web experience for desktop and mobile, cleaner images, original art & printed story, publishing info, and a process clickthrough (pencil, ink, color) of a few pages. I hope you enjoy it.
