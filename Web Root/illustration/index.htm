Illustration

Most of my work was for educational or editorial publishers, with a small percentage of advertsing and corporate. Changing industry demands shifted my tools from traditional media (wet or dry stuff on paper, napkins, motor oil, driftwood, etc.) in the mid-80s to 100% digital by Y2K (even then I used Corel Painter to make my work look less digital). I followed the money to graphic design, which ultimately led to a focused career in web design and development (though I gave it a last gasp with Illustration Friday).
