About me

Twenty-plus years of front-end web development, UX/UI design, graphic design, and illustration for enterprise, publishing, and editorial clients. The work spans the full visual communication stack — from accessible, responsive code to brand systems to print — with a consistent focus on clarity, usability, and craft. Clients and employers include Connecticut Digital Service, Duck Creek Technologies, CVS Health, Merrill Lynch, McGraw-Hill, and Houghton Mifflin.
